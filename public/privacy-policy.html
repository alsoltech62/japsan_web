Privacy Policy for JapsanPay

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to JapsanPay ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs the privacy policies and practices of our application, JapsanPay.

1. Information We Collect

When you use JapsanPay, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and profile details provided during registration.
  • KYC Documents: For verification purposes, we may collect government-issued ID documents (such as Aadhar and PAN cards), especially for vendor registration.
  • Transaction Data: Details of transactions, including the amount of Japsan Coins purchased, transferred, and withdrawn.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your location to help you discover "Nearby Vendors."
  • Camera Access: We request camera access to allow you to scan QR codes for payments and to capture KYC documents.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To verify user identity and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To process payments and manage your Japsan Coin wallet.
  • To show you nearby vendors and localized offers.
  • To improve our app features and user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With Vendors: Necessary transaction details are shared with vendors when you make a payment.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, governmental requests, or to protect our legal rights.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with secure third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., OTP SMS gateways, cloud hosting).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can contact our support team or use the account deletion feature within the app (if available).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
