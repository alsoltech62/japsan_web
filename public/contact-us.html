Frequently Asked Questions (Support)

1. How do I register as a vendor?
Download the JapsanPay app, go to the Profile section, and submit your KYC documents (Aadhar/PAN). Our team will review and approve your vendor profile.

2. My transaction failed but coins were deducted. What should I do?
Please reach out to our support email with your registered phone number and transaction ID. We will resolve the issue within 24-48 hours.

3. How can I withdraw Japsan Coins to my bank account?
Vendors can use the "Withdraw" option in their dashboard to transfer funds to their registered bank account. Standard settlement times apply.
